Biography

  • I am a second year Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Yushu Zhang at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (NUAA).
  • My research focuses on invariant feature extraction and visual signal representation for robust pattern recognition and multimedia-content forensics/security applications.
  • My current work lies in the mathematical foundations of low-level visual tasks, especially the design of theory and implementation of moment invariants. Such algorithms are generic in their nature, leading to potential uses in numerous application areas.

Research Topics
