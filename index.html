Biography

  • I was born in 1997 in Zhumadian, Henan Province. Under my mother's care (Here, thanks to my mother), I lived in my hometown for 18 years until I was admitted to the Computer Science and Technology at Zhengzhou University (2015-2019) through China's National College Entrance Examination. In 2019, I was admitted to the Cyberspace Security at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics through China's Graduate Entrance Examination. I pursued my master's degree under the supervision of Prof. Yushu Zhang and successfully transferred to the doctoral stage in 2022. I am set to graduate in early 2025.
  • Currently, I have joined the School of Computer and Artificial Intelligence at Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics as a lecturer under the leadership of Prof. Yuming Fang.
  • Research interests: Modesty and restraint encryption methods for multimedia content.
    The current field of multimedia encryption typically erases all information indiscriminately to ensure data security, failing to meet the needs of ordinary individuals. As a result, in daily life, people often opt for mosaic-based protection rather than encryption for their multimedia content. That is, ordinary individuals require protection, but not complete protection.
    I considered the conflict between traditional encryption techniques and the needs of ordinary individuals, focusing on developing novel multimedia encryption methods that balance security and usability by retaining coarse information. I refer to this approach as modesty and restraint multimedia encryption.
