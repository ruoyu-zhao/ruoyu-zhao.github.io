Biography

  • I was born in 1997 in Zhumadian, Henan Province. Under my mother's care (Here, thanks to my mother), I lived in my hometown for 18 years until I was admitted to the Computer Science and Technology program at Zhengzhou University (2015-2019) through China's National College Entrance Examination. In 2019, I was admitted to the Computer Science and Technology program at Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics through China's Graduate Entrance Examination. I pursued my master's degree under the supervision of Prof. Yushu Zhang and successfully transferred to the doctoral stage in 2022. I am set to graduate in early 2025.
  • Currently, I have joined the Shool of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics as a lecturer under the leadership of Prof. Fang Yuming's.
  • My research focuses on image encryption and visual signal processing for multimedia security and privacy protection applications.
  • My current work lies in the multimedia visual usability and privacy balance by some encryption protocols.

Research Topics
